Harsco Corporation is a diversified, worldwide company serving industries fundamental to global economic progress and infrastructure development, with more than 400 locations in 50 countries. Harsco acquired Reed Minerals, formerly H.B. Reed, in 1983. Reed Minerals was founded in the 1930's and is considered one America's original "green" companies, recycling coal slag aggregates for beneficial use. Today Reed Minerals continues their commitment to customers under the Harsco name.
